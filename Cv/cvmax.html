About me

I was born on 30 September 1997 in Hasselt, Belgium. my family has a long association with motor sports: my father, Jos Verstappen, is a Dutch former Formula One driver, and my Belgian mother, Sophie Kumpen, competed in karting, and my second cousin, Anthony Kumpen, competed in endurance racing and is a two-time NASCAR Whelen Euro Series champion and currently serves as the team manager for PK Carsport in Euro Series.

Although i have a Belgian mother, was born in Belgium and resided in Bree, Belgium, i decided to compete with a Dutch racing licence because hi feels more Dutch, i spent more time with my father than with my mother owing to his karting activities, and was always surrounded by Dutch people while growing up in Maaseik, a Belgian town at the Dutch border. I competed in Formula One for more than half a season before obtaining a road driver's licence on his 18th birthday. I have lived in Monaco since October 2015, it was not for tax reasons. I do sim racing as a hobby. I won the Team Redline "2020 Real Racers Never Quit" championship that ran in the beginning of 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic. The series ran over multiple rounds on the racing simulator iRacing. I will team up with fellow current Formula One driver Lando Norris on 13-14 June 2020 as drivers for Team Redline at a virtual version of the 24 Hours of Le Mans to be run on rFactor 2.
